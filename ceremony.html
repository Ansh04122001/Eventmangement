A ceremony is an event of ritual significance, performed on a special occasion. The word may be of Etruscan origin, via the Latin caerimonia.

A ceremony may mark a rite of passage in a human life, marking the significance of, for example: initiation (college orientation week), social adulthood (Bar (or Bat) Mitzvah), graduation, awarding, Eretirement, grand opening

a formal religious or public occasion, especially one celebrating a particular event, achievement, or anniversary.
